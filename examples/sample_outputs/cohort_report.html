Xon aperiodic pipeline — cohort report

Recordings processed: 12. This report answers the study's core questions: can the Xon headset recover the aperiodic exponent accurately, is it reliable across a person's repeat sessions, does it survive a noisy condition, and how few minutes of clean data are needed.

Key takeaways

  • Test-retest reliability for movie is ICC(2,1) = 0.9565 (excellent reliability), from 3 participants.
  • Test-retest reliability for rest is ICC(2,1) = 0.988 (excellent reliability), from 3 participants.
  • movie vs rest: exponent differs by -0.0506 on average (n=6 pairs, p=0.0226).
  • 100.0% of recordings reached a stable exponent; median time to stability was 0.25 minutes of clean data - central to the 'a few minutes instead of 8 hours' goal.
  • Across all recordings the mean fit r^2 was 0.9929 (higher = the 1/f slope genuinely fits the spectrum).
